Proud partners in giving back.

We work with likeminded partners to help shape the future with a sharp eye on innovation, equality and sustainability. From women's empowerment to soccer to sponsoring the next generation of leaders in film, learn about what we’re doing at Audi.

Forebs

Since our initial partnership with Forbes in 2017, the “Forbes Idea Incubator” has offered a chance for the next generation’s female STEM leaders to bring their ideas to life. Each year, students at the NYU Tandon School of Engineering work to develop potential solutions to some of the world’s most pressing mobility problems.

Major Leaguae Soccor

Audi is committed to helping modern athletes reach their potential too. Our partnership with Major League Soccer spurred the development of the Audi Player Index. It tracks 88 components and 2,000 player movements to create statistical analysis for each player in each game in near real-time.

Aspen Ski Company

Aspen is the center of the climate change conversation, and Audi is partnering with the city to create a more sustainable future. Audi is helping to electrify the town through a number of projects such as electric public buses, an EV fast charging network and the Audi e-tron at Aspen partner hotels.
